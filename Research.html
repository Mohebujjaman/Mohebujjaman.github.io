Research Interests

 

My research interests lie in finite element analysis and large scale simulations of partial differential equations, which include
  • Newtonian Navier-Stokes equations (NSE)
  • Magnetohydrodynamics (MHD)
  • Fast Algorithms
  • Redued Order Modeling (ROM)
  • Heat and Mass Transfer Flow
  • Maxwell Equations Solver
  • Soft-tissue Deformation Simulation
  • Uncertainty Quantification
  • Sparse Grid Stochastic Collocation Method
