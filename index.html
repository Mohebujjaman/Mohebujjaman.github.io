Welcome to Dr. Mohebujjaman's home.

Currently, I am working as a Postdoctoral Associate at Plasma Science and Fusion Center, Massachusetts Institute of Technology. My primary objective is to build and implement fast efficient algorithms for the Maxwell equations in Petra-M. I am interested in fluid flow simulations, uncertainty quantification (UQ), MHD, finite elements method/analysis, reduced order modeling (ROM), soft-tissue deformation simulation, multi-physics problems, large scale computing, and numerical PDEs in general. I am comfortable in implementing scientific algorithms using
  • Deal.II an open source C++ finite element library
  • Matlab
  • Freefem++
